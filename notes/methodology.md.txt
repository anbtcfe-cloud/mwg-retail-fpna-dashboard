# Methodology

## 1. Data Collection
Financial data was collected from MWG's audited consolidated financial statements, annual reports, and management business plan disclosures.

## 2. Data Processing
Raw financial data was manually extracted and standardized into structured Excel tables. Metrics were checked across years to ensure consistency in units and classification.

## 3. Financial Metrics
The dashboard focuses on:
- Revenue and profit after tax trend
- Gross margin and net margin
- Selling and administrative expense ratios
- Actual vs management plan performance
- Working capital components
- Operating cash flow quality

## 4. Power BI Dashboard
The Power BI dashboard includes three pages:
1. Executive Summary
2. Actual vs Plan
3. Working Capital & Cash Flow

## 5. Design Logic
KPI cards and summary charts use VND trillion for readability, while detailed tables retain VND billion for precision.
